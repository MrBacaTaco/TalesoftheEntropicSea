This mod takes great inspiration from the community’s Calamity: Tales of the Aquatic addon content.

- Features:

17 new items
• 3 new tiles
• 4 weapons
• 1 lore item

9 new enemies
• Including 1 new boss

1 new biome and 1 custom subworld

1 new debuff

- Soundtracks:

Helmet by Philémon Weber (Boss theme)
Ruins of an Ancient Culture by Umplix (Biome theme)
The Beach Where Dreams Die by Chad Wolfe (currently unused)


- References:
