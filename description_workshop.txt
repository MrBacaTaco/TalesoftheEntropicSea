[b][h1]Tales of the Entropic Sea[/h1][/b]

This mod takes great inspiration from the community’s [i]Calamity: Tales of the Aquatic[/i] addon content.

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] 17 new items (including 3 new tiles, 4 weapons, and 1 lore item)
[*] 9 new enemies (including 1 boss)
[*] 1 new biome and 1 subworld
[*] 1 new debuff
[/list]

[h2]Soundtracks[/h2]
[list]
[*] Helmet[/i] by [b]Philémon Weber[/b] – Boss music
[*] Ruins of an Ancient Culture[/i] by [b]Umplix[/b] – Biome music
[*] The Beach Where Dreams Die[/i] by [b]Chad Wolfe[/b] – Currently unused
[/list]
